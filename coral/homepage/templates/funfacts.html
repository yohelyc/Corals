Fun Facts

1. Corals get their rich, florid colors from the bacteria zooxanthellae.

Zooxanthellae and corals have a symbiotic relationship.

2. Corals are actually animals - contrary to popular belief.

However, the bacteria zooxanthellae performs photosynthesis to survive.

3. Corals reefs are one of the most diverse ecosystems in the world.
